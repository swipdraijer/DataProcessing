Life expectancy and GDP

Whilst scrolling through the years, it becomes clear that GDP per capita has always been correlated with life expectancy. Global life expectancy has increased and so did worldwide GDP per capita. However, some countries are an exception to this rule, such as Kuwait with a relatively high GDP per capita but a low average life expectancy and Albania with a relatively high life expectancy despite a low GDP per capita.
